FNAF 3D Simulator
=================

A first-person 3D survival horror game inspired by Five Nights at Freddy's SL, built with C++ and OpenGL on my (best) teacher's template.

The player sits in a security office and must survive against a single, but fierce animatronic by managing doors, lights, and security cameras.

GAMEPLAY
--------
You are locked in a small office. An animatronic moves between the stage and the side corridors. Your goal is to prevent it from reaching you.

Controls:
- Mouse at left edge of screen  -- look left
- Mouse at right edge of screen -- look right
- Space / Tab                     -- open/close security monitor (only when looking forward)
- 1 / 2 / 3                       -- switch camera view on monitor
- E                               -- toggle door
- F (hold)                        -- toggle light

MECHANICS
---------
### Doors
  Doors start OPEN. Press E while looking at a door to close/open it. If a door stays closed for 8 seconds, it opens automatically.

### Lights
  Hold F while facing an open door to shine the hallway light. The light reveals whether the animatronic is standing there.

### Security Monitor
  Open the monitor with Space/Tab while looking straight ahead. Switch between three camera feeds with 1/2/3. Camera 2 shows the corridor; if the animatronic is there, beware of your doors.

### Sounds
  Game has sound cues after certain actions happen in game, follow them for better grasp of the situation
